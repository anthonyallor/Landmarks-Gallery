London Landmarks Gallery

London Eye

Opened in 2000, the London Eye is one of the tallest observation wheels in the world.

Big Ben

The iconic clock tower at the Palace of Westminster, completed in 1859 and symbolizing British democracy.

Tower of London

Built in 1066, this historic castle has served as a palace, prison, and treasury.

Buckingham Palace

The official home of the British monarch since 1837.

Tower Bridge

Opened in 1894, this bridge is one of London’s most recognizable landmarks.

St Paul’s Cathedral

Completed in 1710 and still one of London’s most important churches.

Westminster Abbey

Over 900 years old and used for royal coronations and weddings.

The Shard

Opened in 2012 and is the tallest building in the UK.
